    Welcome to my land!

    I've been building infrastructures for SaaS companies for over 10 years. My goal is a resilient, cost-effective, scalable, maintenance-friendly infrastructure that allows customers to do whatever they need, and allow me and my team to sleep at night. Yes it is possible!

    Building great software means working with smart people to locate and eliminate pain points, aggressively innovate, streamline processes, remove friction, and most importantly, effectively collaborate.

    (Software is already hard, bad communication makes it nearly impossible, and makes it no fun... nobody wants that!

    The Core Principles

    At the end of the day, my DevOps principles are:
    1. Embrace the suck
    2. Fix the problem behind the problem
    3. If you're the smartest person in the room, get into a better room
    4. Innovation is a never-ending process that requires multiple perspectives
    5. Removing friction is the job. Help your customers. Help your team. Help your bosses. Help yourself. The order in non-negotiable.
    6. Have fun. If it's not fun, make it fun.
